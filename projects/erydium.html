ERYDIUM Guitar pedal

This is the ERYDIUM guitar pedal. I've made it as my last electronics project at Summa Engineering. It showcases different circuits that combine to create certain functions. The general functions are:
  • Analog PWM through primitive triangle wave generator with OPAMPs and OPAMP comparator.
  • 20Hz-20kHz AM&FM signal envelope detector.
  • Guitar signal amplitude driven LEDs which light up when guitar is played.
  • Variable frequency blockwave generator made with analog switches to make an electric guitar effect with audio artifacts through aliasing.
  • Distortion effect with multiple transistor stages and potentiometer inputs which affect tone, distortion level and "Bite"
